Warehouse SOPs

Receiving Procedures

  1. When receiving goods, ensure that the purchase order matches the received goods. If there are discrepancies, notify the purchasing department immediately.
  2. Inspect all goods for damage and report any issues to the receiving supervisor.
  3. Once goods have been inspected, check them into inventory and assign a bin location.

Storage Procedures

  1. All goods must be stored in their designated bin location.
  2. Ensure that the weight limit of each bin is not exceeded.
  3. Store goods in a way that allows easy access for picking.

Picking Procedures

  1. Pick orders accurately and efficiently.
  2. Ensure that the correct product and quantity are picked.
  3. Double check all orders before releasing them for shipping.

Shipping Procedures

  1. Verify that the correct product and quantity are being shipped.
  2. Properly package all goods to prevent damage during transit.
  3. Ensure that all shipping documents are accurate and complete.

Inventory Procedures

  1. Conduct regular inventory counts to ensure accuracy.
  2. Investigate and resolve any discrepancies.
  3. Report any inventory issues to the inventory supervisor.

Safety Procedures

  1. Wear appropriate personal protective equipment (PPE) at all times.
  2. Report any safety hazards to the safety supervisor immediately.
  3. Follow all safety procedures outlined in the employee handbook.

By following these procedures, we can ensure a safe and efficient warehouse operation.
